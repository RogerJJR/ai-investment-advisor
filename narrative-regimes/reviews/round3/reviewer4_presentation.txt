STATUS PER ROUND-2 COMMENT
--------------------------
MAJOR 4 (thin Discussion / short Conclusion). RESOLVED. The "Implications for
practice" subsection (lines 548-574) now expands into five labelled items --
(i) quantify before adopting, (ii) preserve regime heterogeneity, (iii)
ablate, don't benchmark, (iv) respect turnover budgets, (v) use as a

rendered as one paragraph, now articulates an empirical replication roadmap
including the "trusted third party to preclude implicit look-ahead during the
development of the filter" language -- the substantive gap is closed.

(lines 394-399) now specifies units: "decimal fractions" for return / vol / DD

addresses the round-2 units complaint. However the caption still carries the
result statement "Narrative Regimes is second-best on Sharpe and CRRA-CE but

belongs in the body, not the caption. Table 3 (tab:snr) column order is

NEW MAJOR COMMENTS (iterated-variant content)
================================================================
1. ATTRIBUTION OF ITERATED GAINS -- HANDLED CORRECTLY.
   Section 4.2 (l.395-416) is admirably disciplined. The +27 bps CRRA-CE lift
   and the Sharpe 0.30 -> 0.32 improvement are explicitly attributed to
   Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage plus 8% annualised vol targeting, with the authors
   stating "The gains are concentrated in the volatility-targeting block: vol
   targeting alone (iteration #15) delivers almost all of the CRRA-CE
   improvement." This matches iteration_log.csv: baseline CRRA-CE 0.02902,
   LW-shrinkage row (iter 9) 0.02999, 8% vol-target row (iter 15) 0.03093;
   every subsequent composite change is marked accepted=False. The authors
   correctly re-frame the variant as "a disciplined two-lever refinement
   (shrinkage + vol targeting) rather than an ensemble of enhancements."
   Crucially, neither the abstract (l.53-56) nor Section 4.2 attributes any
   portion of the iterated gain to the LLM narrative signal. The LLM
   contribution (the +11 pp regime-classification accuracy, Table 3) is
   reported from the baseline configuration only, and is NOT restated or
   augmented in the iterated subsection. This is the correct accounting.

2. TABLE CONSISTENCY WITH REGENERATED CSVs -- VERIFIED.
   - Table 2 (Narrative Regimes row): Sharpe 0.302, MaxDD -0.227, CRRA-CE
     0.0277, turnover 0.075 match table_main.csv row 2 exactly.
   - Table 2 (Iterated row): Sharpe 0.320, Vol 0.0676, MaxDD -0.187,
     CRRA-CE 0.0304, turnover 0.037 match NarrativeRegimesV2 row 3.
   - Table 3 (accuracy): 0.608 / 0.498 match table_accuracy.csv.
   - Table 4 (SNR): accuracy monotone 0.41 -> 0.65, crossover near beta=1,
     consistent with table_snr.csv aggregates.
   No internal inconsistency introduced by the new artefacts.

3. SIGNAL ABSTRACTION UNCHANGED.
   simulate_narrative_signal in simulator.py still emits a one-hot mean plus
   iid Gaussian noise with std = 1/snr; regime_filter.py still applies
   -0.5 * beta * ||s - e_k||^2 as the log-likelihood. The abstraction
   defensibility argument from rounds 2-3 carries over without modification,
   and Section 6.2 (l.614-620) preserves the limitation disclosure verbatim.

4. DIFFERENTIATION FROM PRIOR LLM-PORTFOLIO WORK -- UNAFFECTED.
   The iterated variant does not change the Tang-Zhang taxonomy placement,
   and nothing in Section 4.2 encroaches on LLM-BL (Chen et al. 2025) or
   HARLF (Kim et al. 2025) territory -- vol targeting and LW shrinkage are
   classical allocator techniques unrelated to LLM-side methodology. The
   novelty claim remains the Bayesian filter + regime-policy mix, not the
   vol-targeted wrapper.

5. SELF-FULFILLING-LIKELIHOOD CONCERN -- STILL BACKSTOPPED.
   The iterated variant does not touch the filter, only the allocator, so
   the mis-specified-likelihood robustness result in Appendix A (table_snr_
   misspec with beta* rising from ~1 to ~1.2) remains the relevant evidence.
